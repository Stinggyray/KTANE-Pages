On the Subject of Brutally Beating Broken Buttons

This cheat sheet will help you fixing the broken buttons faster than ever before! Order today!

Perform the first rule that applies, repeat 5 times and then press the correct submit button.

The correct submit button is the LEFT one by default.

The submit button might change for conditions (when condition says Submit = ?)

Condition Actions
“SEA” exists Press it
Any button in 1st or 3rd row starts with “T” Press it
“ONE” + “SUBMIT” both exist Press “ONE” + Submit = Left
Literally blank button Press it
“OTHER” exists Press “OTHER” + Submit = Opposite of the current button
Duplicate words Press one of them
[any port name] + (“PORT" or “MODULE”) exists Press [any port name]
Button with less than 3 characters exists Press it
“BOMB” + “BOOM” Press “BOOM”
“SUBMIT” + “BUTTON” both exist Press correct submit button
“COLUMN” + (“SEVEN” or “TWO”) exist Press “COLUMN”
No buttons have been pressed Press 3rd button in 2nd row
First button pressed had letter “E” Submit = right
N/A --> Just press the correct submit
