On the Subject of Perfectly Plundering Perspective Pegs

Look, no matter how many ways you look at the bomb, it's still going to blow you up if you don't defuse this fast.

  • Positive difference of the 1st and 2nd letters in SN
  • If 4+ letters in SN, add the positive difference of 3rd and 4th letters
  • Look up this number (mod 10) in the Key Color table to get your Key Color.
  • Look for the peg with 3+ of the key color on it, then read the outermost colors from that peg in clockwise order. This is your Current Sequence.
  • Take your current sequence, read through the corresponding column of the Prime/Alternate table, and replace any occurences of a sequence forwards/backwards with the alternate sequence (forwards/backwards).
  • Take the first 3 letters of that sequence, then look for that 3-length color sequence forwards/backwards on any of the 5 perspectives of the pegs. Press the pegs in that order.
